Given information:

Number of apples: 23
Number of oranges: 177
Cost per orange: £4
Total cost of fruit: £869

Cost of oranges = 177 × £4 = £708
Step 2: Calculate the total cost of apples

Total cost of apples = Total cost - Cost of oranges
